How to Play

1. Select a difficulty level and one or more categories (Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division, Advanced).
2. Choose a game mode: Practice, Time Attack (60s), or Endless.
3. Click "Start Game" to begin.
4. Answer the questions by typing your response and clicking "Submit".
5. Use "Hint" if you're stuck (costs points), or "Skip" to move to the next question (also costs points).
6. End the game anytime and check your final score and answers summary.
